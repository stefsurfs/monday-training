Issues/Notes: Your Safety Net
Flag problems, add context, leave notes for teammates
"Waiting on town permit approval before we can proceed"
"Budget needs sign-off from Deputy Director"
"Instructor availability TBD — following up this week"
💡 You don't need to write a lot. A single sentence is enough to keep everyone informed.
Your Monday.com Daily Routine
⏰ Takes less than 5 minutes each morning
Open your team's board
Check your Items and their Progress
Update Progress if anything changed
Flag any blockers in Issues/Notes
💼 Want to manage just your work for today?
Use "My Work" — found in the left-hand sidebar. It pulls together every item assigned to you across all boards into one personal view, so you can focus on what you need to get done today without switching between boards.
That's it. Leadership can now see your progress without interrupting your work.

Quick Reference Cheat Sheet
Daily (2-5 min): Check board, update Progress
Weekly (5-10 min): Review all items, update ETAs
When Blocked: Change status → Blocked + add note
When Done: Change status → Completed
Need Help: Add @mention in Issues/Notes
Every update you make saves someone from having to ask you for it later.
